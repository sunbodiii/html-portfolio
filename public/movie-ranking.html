Sol's Favorite Flicks!

Here are a few of my favorite movies

The Lion King

This is a childhood favorite of mine, one of the first movies I can recall loving. I'm also partially biased since my zodiac sign is Leo.

Blood and Bone

This is a gritty martial arts movie starring Michael Jai White and it's packed full of fighting scenes and action from front to back. As a martial arts enthusiast myself, it is truly inspiring to me.

They Cloned Tyrone

Man, this movie is a rollercoaster. The concept of cloning is tackled marvelously along with goverment conspiracies and social experimentation. I was NOT disappointed in this movie.
