Sol's Favorite Flicks

Here are some of my favorite movies :)

The Lion King

One of the most memorable movies from my childhood. It has a special place in my heart because my zodiac sign is Leo, and I love the songs they sing throughout the film.

Ong-Bak

I am a martial arts enthusiast, and this movie right here has some of the best choreographed stunts I've ever seen. Very authentic. Extremely true to the core of the muay thai martial art.

Paprika

This movie takes you on a journey into the mind! If you're familiar with the movie Inception, then behold! A lot of the plot and premise of that movie is taken directly from this one, and in my opinion, this movie is better. It appeals to me more, plus I'm biased towards animated films.
